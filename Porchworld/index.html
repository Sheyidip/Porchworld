About Us

PorchWorld Global Investment Limited is a leading paint manufacturing company specializing in high-quality paints for homes, offices, restaurants, and more. Our product, PorchPaint, is designed to bring beauty and durability to every surface.

Founded in 2011 by Mr Akinwale Ogundipe, a visionary in the architectural design industry, PorchWorld has grown to become a trusted name in the market. We have successfully completed projects in several cities, delivering excellence in both residential and commercial spaces.

Our mission is to provide innovative, eco-friendly, and long-lasting paint solutions that inspire creativity and transform spaces.
